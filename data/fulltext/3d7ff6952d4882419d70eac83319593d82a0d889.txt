Abstract
The expansion of the pharmacist‑led services has resulted in increased demand to have evidence in terms of neces‑
sity, efficacy, and cost. Evidence‑based practice is crucial to provide the best patient health outcomes and pharmacy
practice research can play a key role in this regard. This commentary provides a background of pharmacy practice
research, and then, it highlights three key recommendations based on a systematic review of the literature. The
systematic review of the literature on pharmacy practice research has been conducted in 12 Middle Eastern Arab
Countries. The three key recommendations include (a) identification of research priorities by health authorities in each
country; (b) pharmacy practice research culture to be nurtured and promoted with academic researchers and practi‑
tioners; and (c) shifting pharmacy practice research towards applied, interventional, and implementation studies
Keywords: Pharmacy practice research, Evidence‑based practice, Middle East, Arab countries, Recommendation
